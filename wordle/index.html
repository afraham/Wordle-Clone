Wordle

How to Play:

  • Start typing, the letters will appear in the boxes
  • Remove letters with a Backspace
  • Hit Enter/Return to submit an answer
  • Letters with a Green background are in the right spot!
  • Letters with a Yellow background exist in the word, but are in the wrong spot(s)
  • Letters with a Gray background don't exist in the word
If a hint is needed, press the ? icon
For dark mode, press the ◑ icon
